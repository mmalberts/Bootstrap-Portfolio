About Me

I am a current student of the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp, working toward gaining the skills necessary to become a fullstack developer. I am a board-certified music therapist, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Music Therapy from the University of Iowa.

Based in Chicago, I spend a lot of time exploring the city and all it has to offer. I travel as frequently as I can, and the rest of my free time is spent doing triathlons, rock climbing, learning new instruments, and working on various creative projects.
